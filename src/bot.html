The honest verdict first

VERIFIED Naafiri bot (carry position) is a legitimate niche pick, not a troll pick — but the data is thin and split: lolalytics has her at 52.8% over 1,290 Emerald+ games; skill-capped's cut says 48.0% over 999. Truth: somewhere in "roughly even, playable when conditions are right." Pick it when the enemy botlane is immobile and low-poke; don't first-pick it. Naafiri support is a troll pick: 43.3% over 1,059 games — the one role verdict in this project that isn't close.

1 · Why it works — and why it sometimes doesn't

REASONING (no site publishes duo-lane data at this sample size, so this section is mechanics, labeled as such):

  • Works: 900-range Q poke outranges every ADC's autos; packmates eat the skillshots that supports throw; Gluttonous omnivamp + Q heal out-sustains most 2v2 poke; and post-6, a point-and-click 900-range R onto an ADC — in a lane where both targets are squishy — is a permanent 2-for-0 threat. The R-reset weave (§1b mid) is better in a 2v2 than anywhere else: two kills are standing next to each other.
  • Doesn't: duo XP delays level 6 by ~2–3 minutes — and post-midscope, level 6 is her entire engage pattern (the dash is her R). Pre-6 she is a poke bully with no gap closer and no escape past E. Artillery poke she can't dodge (Ziggs, Senna, Brand support) drains her before 6 ever matters, and hyper-all-in duos (Samira, Nilah + engage support) fight her at level 2 where she's weakest.

4 · Duo dynamics REASONING

  • Best supports: engage with CC that holds still (Leona, Nautilus, Rell) — their chain → your R is a guaranteed connect, and the takedown arms your recast onto the second target. The R-reset weave is the whole 2v2 win condition post-6.
  • Also fine: poke mages (Xerath, Lux) who make the lane a 900-range shooting gallery you're already best at.
  • Awkward: enchanters — you don't auto often enough to carry their buffs; and if the enemy runs an enchanter, buy Serpent's Fang (50% shield cut) by 3rd–4th.
  • Lane rhythm: pre-6, play it like your mid poke war (§6 mid) with a bodyguard: Q on their support's engage cooldowns, W held for their all-in button. Post-6, every wave-crash owes the map the same §6 decision — except your "roam" is usually the drake pit 20 seconds away.

Support Naafiri — the data says no VERIFIED

43.3% WR over 1,059 games; her best support rune page wins 44%. No gold = no lethality spikes = a champion whose entire kit scales on bonus AD, with no bonus AD. The one-line verdict: if you want Naafiri and got autofilled support, that's what role swaps and dodges are for.
