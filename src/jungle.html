1 · Why She Jungles Now

VERIFIED ~69% of Naafiri's games are jungle on 26.13: S/S- tier, 50.5–52.3% WR Emerald+, top-10 jungler on lolalytics, 15–27% ban rate. The history: patch 26.03 restored her passive's monster damage to 155% (Riot explicitly said it was to help her clear), which moved her home to the jungle; 26.04 trimmed early mana/HP-regen sustain, and 26.10 shaved Q bleed damage and R base damage. Nerfed twice, still strong.

2b · Comp Adjustments REASONING

Same audit as the mid guide (§3b), jungle flavor: (1) 2+ tanks → the Cleaver hybrid above IS the answer — and it's your Fatality slot, so no Serylda's alongside it. (2) Your team all squishy → you still can't be their frontline; Naafiri doesn't buy tank items, she picks from fog instead of fighting front-to-back — EoN/DD climb a slot. (3) 3+ hard-CC comp → the Mercs line (Legend: Tenacity was removed in 14.10; boots are your tenacity now). (4) Aatrox/Mundo/Warwick on their side → a solo laner buys the GW, not you — your slots are kill tools, and one GW per team is the rule.

3 · The Clear

VERIFIED The one mechanic that defines her jungle: Q's cooldown starts only after Q2 (or when the 4s recast lapses). Between camps with no downtime, recast Q2 immediately so it's back up for the next camp; delay Q2 only when you need the full bleed detonation. Landing both casts matters everywhere — missing one drastically lowers her damage.

REASONING No reliably-sourced 26.13 clear route exists — published routes predate the 26.10 passive trim. Practical approach: full-clear toward the side you intend to gank first, and time your route once in practice tool; sub-3:30 full clears are the benchmark for most AD junglers.

4 · Early Game: Respect the Weakness

VERIFIED She's weak levels 1–5: slower early clear, no escape tools, loses early duels — enemy junglers are told to punish her exactly then. So: don't contest early Scuttle into strong duelists, full-clear, hit your item spike (1–2 items), then flip aggressive. Post-Voltaic she wins most 1v1s — that's the Conqueror synergy and your cue to skirmish over every objective.

5 · Ganking & Snowballing

  • VERIFIED Post-6 ganks are her identity: R is a 900-range point-and-click dive with a takedown recast — gank lanes with CC, kill, recast onto the second laner. Fast roam/pack mobility spreads leads across the map; isolated targets are the diet.
  • VERIFIED Snowball levers: (1) picking up early kills → pivot rush from Voltaic to Hubris to compound gold; (2) Voltaic completing is the green light to force skirmishes and invade; (3) chain takedowns — R recast + Axiom Arc refunds turn one kill into multi-kill fights; (4) convert every gank into an objective (Dragon after bot ganks, Herald/grubs after top).
  • REASONING Same Hubris rule as mid: it needs guaranteed takedowns to out-value Voltaic's unconditional proc. 2–3 kills before first full item = Hubris window; even game = Voltaic.

6 · Matchups (Jungle)

VERIFIED The verified matchup table covers junglers only — Bel'Veth, Briar, Kayn, Nocturne, Hecarim, Rammus are the current meta opponents tracked on u.gg. REASONING Principles: early-duel monsters (Bel'Veth, Briar, Lee) beat you levels 1–5 — trade map-side, don't fight; scaling/farm junglers you out-tempo after Voltaic; Rammus is the classic "your whole kit is physical" counter — 92% physical damage means armor stackers blunt you; go Serylda's earlier or the Cleaver hybrid (one, not both — they share the Fatality shop limit).
